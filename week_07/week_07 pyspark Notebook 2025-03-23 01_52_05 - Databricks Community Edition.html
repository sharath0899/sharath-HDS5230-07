    CONCLUSION: Analysis of 2023 CMS Open Payments data reveals the extensive range of monetary transactions that occurs between medical practitioners and industry production companies. The processing of extensive datasets became efficient through our utilization of PySpark on Databricks which revealed important findings.

    Among the total 13 million Food and Beverage payments the individual transactions typically amounted to minimal dollar values.

    Royalty or License payments held fewer records than other categories yet delivered the highest total payment figure that reached $1.19 billion thus signifying substantial worth of intellectual property agreements.

    Orthopaedic Surgery in combination with Neurology and Internal Medicine collected the most significant payments with their services along with consulting and education activities.

    Medical practitioners yielded substantial financial rewards reaching tens of millions of dollars due to their industry-based innovation involvement and royalty payments and leadership roles.

    Payments to hospitals and research institutions and group accounts were frequently recorded as null values in specialty fields along with physician name fields.

    This research project proves how PySpark enables large-scale healthcare analysis while showing why medical providers need to maintain financial transparency with their industry counterparts. The discovered findings provide essential knowledge to develop healthcare policies while enhancing compliance efforts and future academic medical research.
